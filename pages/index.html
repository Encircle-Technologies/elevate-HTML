Welcome to Elevate your chair

We're redefining what it means to grow as a hairstylist.

Created by the award-winning team at Blake Charles Salon, Elevate Your Chair is your home for real education, real connection, and real transformation.

This isn’t just another class platform—it’s a daily source of fuel for your creativity, your business, and your mindset. Whether you're just starting out or leveling up, we’re here to walk with you—every step, every stage, every day.

What makes us different? We focus on the wellness of the stylist behind the chair. Our mission is rooted in personal growth, emotional resilience, and purpose-driven careers.

We offer more than classes. We offer support, mentorship, and clarity—including Therapy Tuesdays, a monthly video series featuring therapists and mindset coaches discussing real topics that affect hairstylists—from burnout and anxiety to confidence and communication skills.
